# Project Criteria for Moving Levels

Projects exist within the CNCF in one of 3 maturity levels that indicate their expected characteristics, functionality, and assurances to potential adopters. Archived projects do not meet the maturity level criteria for a variety of reasons. More information on archived projects may be found in the [Archive](archiving.md) doc.

## Understanding the Criteria Tables

The Criteria tables are intended to express all the criteria for moving levels, which level they apply, if there is an usual or common location where the content is found, and how the primary owner for completing the criteria. They are broken up by topic to make the criteria more consumable for projects.

As the TOC continuously refines the criteria, additional notes may be added to the table to capture any areas needing additional clarity.
 
Some criteria may include sub-criteria that indicate the criteria is met through completion of a process or action. These are visible in the table by the `^^` preceeding the criteria and the Primary Owner field.
